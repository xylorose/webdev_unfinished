Korean drama or K-drama refers to televised dramas in Korean language, made in South Korea,
mostly in a miniseries format, with distinctive features that set it apart from regular Western television series or soap operas.
Korean dramas can be set in contemporary times or in historical settings, the Korean word for the latter being sageuk.

Supernatural Korean Movie

Vampires, witches, aliens, ghosts, etc. The otherness of these romances creates wonderful tension and obstacles without all that unnecessary angst. Romantic Comedy Movies

The genre of romantic comedy is perfect when you're looking for a very cute and funny drama! It has a lot of funny moments and a lot of romance, just like the title says! Romantic comedy dramas are dramas with light-hearted, humorous plotlines, centered on romantic ideals such as that true love is able to surmount most obstacles.

School Korean Movie

This drama usually revolves around the lives of one high school student, or maybe even more. We see their struggles, their relationships with others, and how they develop into mature adults while going through common problems high school students face, like bullying and suicide attempts.

Action Korean Movie

If you are into the type of dramas that involve a lot of exciting stunts, dramatic twists and turns, car chases, fights, battles, ect, then this is the genre for you. These tend to be a lot more exciting and less romantic and funny then a Rom Com or melodrama, but they still sometimes have romance!
